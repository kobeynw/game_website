    Daze Town

    A Wild West Adventure

    Daze Town is a 2D platformer game set in the heart of the Wild West! The game revolves around the journey of a rugged, bare-bones outsider who makes his way through casinos, mineshafts, and tattoo parlors. The game's theme, characters, and even the title itself are heavily inspired by the developers' backgrounds. Both Workman brothers grew up in a small rural town in Nevada named Dayton, originally called Day's Town.

    Style and Function

    Daze Town is created using the Unity 2D Engine, which provides the flexibility and functionality needed to make a beautiful game. Kobey Workman uses his computer programming know-how to make the game elements and levels, while Oakley Workman utilizes his fine-tuned graphic design skills for the sprites and other artwork assets. With the AI industry boom, possibilities for ideation and creative assistants helped shape and expedite some of the game’s look, functionality, and feel, including the music.
