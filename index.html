    Daze Town

    A Wild West Adventure

    Daze Town is a 2D platformer game set in the heart of the Wild West! The game revolves around the journey of a rugged, bare-bones outsider who makes his way through casinos, mineshafts, and tattoo parlors.

    From the Roots

    The game's theme, characters, and even the title itself are heavily inspired by the developers' backgrounds. Both grew up in a small rural town in Nevada named Dayton, originally called Day's Town.
